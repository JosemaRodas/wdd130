History!

We are a new and exciting rafting company here in La Paz, Bolivia, driven by a shared passion for adventure and a deep appreciation for the natural wonders that surround us.

This is just the beginning of our story. As we embark on this exciting journey, we look forward to welcoming you on board and creating unforgettable memories etched in the heart of the Bolivian rapids.

Rafting Adventure La Paz is a new and exciting company offering a gateway to the heart-pounding thrills and breathtaking landscapes of Bolivia's rivers. We're driven by a deep appreciation for the natural world and a shared passion for adventure. This is more than just a trip; it's an opportunity to create lasting memories. Whether you're a seasoned adventurer or a curious beginner, we have something for everyone. Conquer the class V rapids of the Mapiri River, explore the lush scenery of the Coroico River, or enjoy a tranquil wildlife haven on the Beni River. Our commitment goes beyond providing an exhilarating experience. We prioritize safety with expert guides and high-quality equipment. Furthermore, we're dedicated to minimizing our impact on the environment, ensuring the beauty of these rivers can be enjoyed for generations to come. Embark on an unforgettable journey with Rafting Adventure La Paz. We're your gateway to a world of excitement and unique experiences in the heart of Bolivia.
